Old Business:

  • Helping the Canon City club with repairs to their repeater in lieu of helping Jeff with his repeaters was discussed and Amanda (K1DDN) said Jeff declined any help. Discussion was held on the Canon City club tower being in trouble and they could use help with cables and tower work. The info available was that they have tower sections to use so it is possible they might need help with making new cables. WA0NLK is the person to talk to about helping them.

  • Discussion was held about the new repeater being linked for the simplex net so many other people can check in. The purpose of the simplex net is to test the ability of everyone to be able to communicate in case of an emergency. Simplex nets also help everyone to practice the use of relays in communicating. Simplex is simplex. The point was brought up that linking the repeaters can be a problem with delays and people not realizing the problems caused by linking. Some frustration has been caused by the problems with linking.

  • Net etiquette was also discussed. It was suggested a simple document be written about proper simplex net use to be sent out to all members.

  • The suggestion was made to alternate the repeater and simplex nets. No action was taken.

  • The suggestion was made to have the names of the persons checking into the nets put into a drawing every couple of months. The prizes could be gift certificates or things like that. This will be discussed further.
