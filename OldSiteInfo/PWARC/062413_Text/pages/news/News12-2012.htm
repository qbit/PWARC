Coming Events:

  • Each Tuesday - 7:00 p.m. - First session of the PWARC net on the Royal Gorge Ham Club repeater at Canon City (145.490 [-] PL103.5).

  • Each Thursday - 7:00 p.m. - Test for back-up net frequency on 147.000 (+), PL 100. Main purpose is to see how many club members can hit this repeater, especially those in Pueblo and east of there.
