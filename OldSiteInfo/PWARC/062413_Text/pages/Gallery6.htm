Water Donation

On October 30, 2012, the Pueblo West Amateur Radio Club presented the Pueblo West Fire Department with 10 cases of bottled drinking water. The donation was a token of the club's appreciation for the firemens' work in the community and for the Department providing a site for the club's annual ARRL Field Day event in June.

The firemen were very grateful for the donation, as there is a continuing need for bottled drinking water as the volunteers fight fires in Pueblo West and outside the immediate area.

 

September Antenna Party

Neal (KD0MBL), Jim (NE0DA), Paul (KB0JIT) and Steve (KD0QCS) and Stan (KC0OYO) made quick work of raising the dipoles at the home of Bill and Jane (WA0KAQ and KA0USA) on Sept. 20. Three push-up poles replaced 15' PVC pipe that had supported the dipoles for about 8 years. The dipoles are now at about 25'. Bill noted that this is the first time the 80 and 40 meter dipoles had been off the roof.
