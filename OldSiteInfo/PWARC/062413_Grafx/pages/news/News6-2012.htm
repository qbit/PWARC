ARRL Membership Report:

Pueblo West Amateur Radio Club is an ARRL Affiliated Club, and we encourage our members to join the ARRL. The dues amount to a little more than $3.00 a month for an individual membership, and the information received from the monthly QST magazine you receive is well worth the price.

To retain our affiliation, the club needs to maintain a 51% ARRL membership. For that, the club may retain $2 of the annual ARRL dues, and $15 for each new membership. If you are joining ARRL for the first time or are renewing, please do it through the club so that some of your payment will go to our club's treasury. See WA0KAQ for application forms, and return them to him when you have filled them out.

Following the meeting, the club has 37 members, 36 of whom are licensed. Of those 10 hold Technician licenses, 7 have General licenses, 3 hold Advanced licenses, and 16 have Amateur Extra licenses. Of the 36 voting members, 22 belong to ARRL (61%).
