    • Dan, Jane and David will meet at 10:00 Tuesday, Jan. 17, to sign the signature card.

  • Member Bill Dietrick, KD0ICM, had been having a problem with his two meter antenna. KB0JIT and KD0MBL met with Bill and found the problem to be that the wind had loosened the antenna, allowing it to slip lower on the mast. The antenna was checked out with an antenna analyzer and raised on its mast. The antenna is now providing good service. Thank you, Paul and Neal, for helping out a fellow club member.

Program:

Ed, NI5R, presented a very interesting program on building solar panels. He is currently in the process of building several and brought solar cells and a panel frame to show. A video was also shown which described the process of connecting the cells together and securing them to the glass.
