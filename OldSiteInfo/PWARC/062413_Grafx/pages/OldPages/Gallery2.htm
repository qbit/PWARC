PWARC First Anniversary Party
Saturday, August 28, 2010

Pueblo West Amateur Radio Club celebrated its First Anniversary on August 28 at the home of Bill (KB0PRD) and Debbie Fortune with a covered dish supper. Ralph, KA0RER (L) brought some delicious smoked ribs for the main dish, and Donna Kelley (Bottom L) baked and decorated the anniversary cake. The group relaxed before the dinner, then retired to the Fortune's new deck to eat.

KB0RPD thanked Bill and Jane Ewing (WA0KAQ and KA0USA respectively) for forming the club. The group sang Happy Birthday to the club, and club Vice-president Andy, ND0P, blew out the candles on the cake.

Thank you Bill and Debbie for your hospitality.

Colorado 14er Event
Pikes Peak - August 2010

Having a new ham license spurred Neal , KD0MBL, to explore mountain top communications during the 2010 Colorado 14er event. Andy, ND0P, and Andy's son Benji (6) accompanied Neal. The two hams are the first from Pueblo West Amateur Radio Club to participate in the event.

Using a Yaesu FT-857, a homebrew 20 meter dipole used as an inverted "V" with a fishing pole for a mast, and using two 120-watt solar panels, the two had an enjoyable outing. Paul, KB0JIT, helped Neal build the antenna they used.

Bob, K0NR, from Monument was working the event on VHF/UHF, and shared his experience with them.
