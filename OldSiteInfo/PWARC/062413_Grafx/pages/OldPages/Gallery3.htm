Destination Imagination Science Fair Competition
March 19, 2011

 
Lori, AH6OS (left) and Jane, KA0USA represented PWARC when they joined with several members of Pueblo Ham Club to provide communications for Destination Imagination at CSU-Pueblo on Saturday, March 19.

The annual competition features creative science projects made by young students from throughout the state.

Four Members Attended Quartzfest 2011, Jan. 23-30, near Quartzsite, AZ

Quartzfest is an annual gathering of ham radio operators from across the nation and Canada that "boondock" in the desert while holding their hamfest. This was the first time for both couples to attend the event.

PWARC First Antenna Party
Saturday, September 11, 2010

PWARC held its first Antenna Party on September 11 at the home of WA0KAQ & KA0USA. Paul, KB0JIT, brought samples of several VHF antennas helped the participants build their antennas. Neal, KD0MBL, and Andy, ND0P, (below left) built 2-meter J-Pole antennas for portable work from 300-ohm TV lead in wire. David, KD0MQB, (below right) brought a homebrew 2-meter beam he was in the process of building and took home the completed project.
