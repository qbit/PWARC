PWARC Celebrates Third Anniversary

No one went home hungry when the Pueblo West Amateur Radio Club held its Third Anniversary Party on Wednesday, August 22, at Pueblo West Library. The covered dish supper drew 20 hungry members, spouses and guests.

PWARC President Jane Ewing was caught with two plates, but she said one was for husband Bill. HI HI

PWARC Presents Elmer Award to WA0KAQ

Neal Tew (L), KD0MBL, presented Bill Ewing, WA0KAQ, with an ARRL Elmer Award at the club's August 11, 2012 meeting. Neal stated the award was being presented to the club's founder, although he is grateful for everyone's assistance through the last two years and for the fun he has had during that time.

Bill thanked Neal and told the members that he is proud of every one of them. "Almost every day I hear how nice the members are and how helpful the members have been to other hams, whether or not they are members. This is a great bunch of hams we have here. I'm proud of all of you for what you've done."

Photos by Neal Tew, KD0MBL

449.275 Repeater Node

K0JSC installed a UHF repeater node in Pueblo West on August 6, 2012. The node links to Canon City and Pueblo and can be linked to other repeaters in the area.

KY6YYY Presents WA0KAQ With "Memory Book"

David Yeater, KY6YYY (seated right) presented Bill Ewing, WA0KAQ, with a beautiful "Memory Book" at the July 14 PWARC meeting.

David included photos of Bill & Jane, PWARC members, family members, friends awards received, events Bill attended, and other club activities. The book is very much appreciated.

Mishelle Oakley Receives First PWARC Scholarship

It gives Pueblo West Amateur Radio Club great pleasure to present its first annual scholarship in the amount of $250 to Michelle Oakley.

Michelle is a 2012 graduate of Pueblo West High School and will attend Pueblo Community College, majoring in Biology and minoring in Education.

Michelle won many honors and academic awards in math, science and band during her time in high school.

In her application, Michelle stated, "My dream is to become a teacher! I can not wait for the moments ahead when I achieve this. Once I start teaching Biology in high school, I plan to make science fun for my students."
