Basic Club Emergency Plan

1) Every member should strive to make back up power available for his station.

2) Every member should know how to program and use his/her radio. Practice, practice, practice!

4) In the event of power failure, telephone outage, storms:

  • Change to battery power if necessary.

  • Change to indoor antenna if lightning is present.

  • Set antenna as far as possible away from operating position and keep power down to minimum needed.

  • Tune to 146.445 Simplex.

  • If you hear no one after a few minutes, give a short call.

  • To conserve battery power, monitor for 5 minutes on the hour and half hour for updates.

5) If the club's net is in session, you may check in when Net Control Station (NCS) requests check-ins. When checking into a net, keep transmissions short. Afterwards, do not transmit unless directed to do so by the NCS. The only exception to this rule is if you have emergency traffic for the net.

6) Plan Review

  • This PWARC Community Communications Plan shall be reviewed at least annually, and updates may be made at any time to keep this plan current and viable.
