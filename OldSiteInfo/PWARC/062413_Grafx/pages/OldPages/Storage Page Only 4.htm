Directions to Field Day Site:

.

.From the U.S. Hwy. 50/South McCulloch Blvd. intersection (Walmart will be on the northeast corner), turn left if you are coming from Pueblo, or right if you are coming from Canon City, onto S. McCulloch Blvd.

There is a stoplight one block ahead of you and gas stations on the right. Continue through this intersection and work your way into the left lane. You will see a small park and lake on your right. Vectra Bank will be on your left. A stop light is at S. Joe Martinez Blvd.

Turn left on S. Joe Martinez Blvd. You will pass the Pueblo West Library and the Pueblo County Sheriff's Station on your left. The next building on your left is the Pueblo West Fire Station. Turn left on Hahns Peak Avenue, then left into the fire station. Park out of the way behind the building.
