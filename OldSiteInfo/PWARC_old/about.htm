About Pueblo West Amateur Radio Club, Inc. was founded on August 15, 2009 and is a 501(c)(3) nonprofit corporation registered with the State of Colorado. Unless otherwise noted, PWARC meetings are held at 2:00 p.m. on the second Saturday of each month at the Pueblo West Library, 298 S. Joe Martinez Blvd., Pueblo West. Anyone interested in learning about the Amateur Radio hobby and Emergency Communications is welcome to attend our meetings and Ragchews.
