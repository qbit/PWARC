SCHOOL ROUNDUP

Skyview Middle School
February 18, 2011

Photographs by KA0USA, KB0JIT and KY6YYY

Pueblo West Amateur Radio Club participated in its first ARRL School Roundup at Skyview Middle School in Pueblo West on February 18, 2011. Although the student turnout was light, those who participated were given an overview of amateur radio and talked with individual and club stations from coast to coast. They commented that they enjoyed the one-on-one attention they received

Club members participating included Jane (KA0USA), David (KY6YYY), Bill (KB0RPD), Paul (KB0JIT), Ed (NI5R), and Andy (NDOP).

The club is planning to expand the event when the School Roundup is held again in October.
