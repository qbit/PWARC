Old Business:

  • At the October meeting, members approved the purchase of 10 cases of bottled water to be donated to the Pueblo West Fire Department. David, KY6YYY, and Jane, KA0USA, delivered the water, which was much appreciated by the firemen.

  • The Christmas party and potluck dinner on December 8 will start about 3 p.m. following the meeting. Carrie, KD0SOV, passed around a sign up sheet for items being brought to the potluck. Members of the Pueblo Ham Club and Royal Gorge Ham Club members will be invited.

  • Net Manager Steve Worley, KD0QCF, reported that the Thursday night net on 147.000 has been averaging 14 people. The Tuesday night nets have been having about 9-10 check-ins on both the145.490 and simplex nets. Club members east of Pueblo have been able to participate in the Thursday night net, whereas they have had problems getting into the Tuesday night net. He noted that this is not an attempt to get rid of the net on Tuesday. The frequency used on Thursdays could serve as a back-up net or resource net if needed. In a big emergency, we would use anything available. Discussion on the future of the Thursday night net was tabled until the December meeting.
