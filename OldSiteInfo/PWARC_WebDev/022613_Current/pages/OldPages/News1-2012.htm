Program: (NI5R )

  • This was the bulk of our meeting discussion. Ed was ready to start some classes and set up time for it after our monthly meeting or at another time. Most people were in favor of having it right after the regular meeting. The talk then turned to what would our roll would be in an emergency and who would want to use our services. Amanda, Neal, and Stan said that the emergency services in Pueblo and Fremont counties don't want amateur radio help. So then the discussion went to who would want our help, what would our role be, and then what do we need to learn and do to fill that roll.

  • Maybe we should ask clubs in the Springs about what they are doing to fill this roll.

  • Another item brought up was what would be the best way to send messages between stations. Stan suggested that packet would be the best way to do this.

  • Neal suggested that we might want to use standard operating procedures like FEMA uses just so we might integrate easier.

  • In the end it was decided to table the issue until we determine our place and roll in an emergency com situation. Once that is determined then we will be better able to train properly for the roll we want to fill.

  • There was agreement that this was important and worth doing. So bring all your knowledge, ideas, suggestions, and comments to the next meeting!
