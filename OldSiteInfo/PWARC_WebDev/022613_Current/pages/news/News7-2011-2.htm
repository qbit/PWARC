Liberty Point 5K Run

  • WA0KAQ, AH6OS, KD0MBL, KY6YYY, KC6ELM, NE0DA and K0JSC provided back-up communications for the Liberty Point 5K Run/Walk on July 4. The event was sponsored by United Way of Pueblo County, Pueblo West Metropolitan District and the Pueblo West Chamber of Commerce. In addition to our main purpose, we were asked to do minor traffic control and report walkers were running on the course.

    • Good Points:

      • We had a safe event with eight members participating.

      • Biance Hicks of United Way of Pueblo County, was very impressed with our having a written plan, including maps of where our members would be located.
      • Our spacing of stations along the route turned out to be very good for communications.
      • Everyone made a smooth transition to our alternate frequency when we experienced interference on the main channel.
      • When WA0KAQ's mobile antenna refused to work, he had a spare on hand.
      • Our help was greatly appreciated, and WA0KAQ was told we will be invited to participate again next year.

    • Things that need improvement:

      • Equipment needs to be checked in advance of the event.

      • Some members need to be more familiar with using their handhelds and the features their radios have.
      • We had a problem with an open microphone.
      • Our use of tactical calls needs to be improved upon -- by all of us. We hadn't had any practice though. We will do better next time.

    • Over all, it was a very successful exercise.
